Luchi with Cholar Dal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1–2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt (a pinch)
  • Warm water (to knead)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, salt, and ghee.
  2. Add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Rest 20–30 mins.
  4. Divide into small balls and roll into small circles.
